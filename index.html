ABOUT ME

Patricia S Wilkins

CEO/President

Patricia S. Wilkins is nationally recognized as an expert in Workforce Development and Civic Engagement. She is sought for her expertise regarding the administration of federal and state economic development programs.

Most recently, Patricia served as the Vice President of Workforce Development at National Able Network. National Able Network is an organization that helps job seekers of all ages, skills and income levels achieve economic self-sufficiency, and provides human resources, diversity & inclusion and other solutions to companies of all sizes.

In this role, she was the driving force behind the strategy, research, development and implementation of the Senior Community Service Employment Program (SCSEP) and the organization’s workforce development programs. During her tenure, she was recognized by the United States Department of Labor for her professional achievements and exceptional performance.

Before joining National Able, Patricia spearheaded project management efforts for technology, social services, communications and Fortune 500 companies and organizations.

Patricia also serves on the Board of Directors for ProjectGo, a community organization with a mission to strengthen the partnerships between families, schools and community leaders. Additionally, she maintains active involvement in her local community by volunteering at community-based organizations that focuses on education, women empowerment and economic development.

Patricia earned her BA from Columbia College in Communications, her Masters Certification in Project Management from Stevens Institute of Technology and is currently pursuing her Master’s Degree in Public Administration from Cornell University.
